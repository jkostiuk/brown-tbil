Exercise 2. GT2.

Show how to compute the determinant of the matrix
\begin{equation*} A = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 3 & 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 3 \\ -3 & 1 & 2 & -5 \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}
🔗
Solution.
Here is one possible solution, first applying a single row operation, and then performing Laplace/cofactor expansions to reduce the determinant to a linear combination of \(2\times 2\) determinants. First, subtracting the second row from the first and then expanding along the second row, we have
\begin{align*} \det A \amp= \det \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 3 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 3 \\ -3 & 1 & 2 & -5 \end{array}\right] \\ \amp= (-1) \det \left[\begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 3 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 & 3 \\ -3 & 1 & -5 \end{array}\right] + (1) \det \left[\begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 3 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 2 \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{align*}
🔗
Next we compute
\begin{align*} \det \left[\begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 3 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 & 3 \\ -3 & 1 & -5 \end{array}\right] \amp= (1) \det \left[\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 3 \\ 1 & -5 \end{array}\right] - (1) \det \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 1 & -5 \end{array}\right] + \\ \amp \phantom{=}~ (-3) \det \left[\begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array}\right] \\ \amp= (1)(-8)-(1)(-14)+(-3)(10)\\ \amp=-24 \end{align*}
and
\begin{align*} \det \left[\begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 3 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 2 \end{array}\right] \amp = (1) \det \left[\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array}\right] - (3) \det \left[\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 2 \end{array}\right]\\ \amp = 1-15\\ \amp=-14\text{,} \end{align*}
so
\begin{gather*} \det A =-1(-24)+(1)(-14)=10\text{.} \end{gather*}
🔗
Here is another possible solution, using row and column operations to first reduce the determinant to a \(3\times 3\) matrix and then applying a formula:
🔗
\begin{align*} \det \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 3 & 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 3 \\ -3 & 1 & 2 & -5 \end{array}\right] \amp= \det \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 3 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 3 \\ -3 & 1 & 2 & -5 \end{array}\right] \\ \amp= \det \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 3 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 2 \\ -3 & 1 & 2 & -7 \end{array}\right]\\ \amp=-\det \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 3 & 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\ -3 & 1 & 2 & -7 \end{array}\right] \\ \amp= -\det \left[\begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 3 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 & 2 \\ -3 & 1 & -7 \end{array}\right]\\ \amp=-((-7-18-1)-(3+2-21))\\ \amp=10 \end{align*}
