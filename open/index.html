Open Education

To maximize the impact of our work, we offer our TBIL Resource Library as free and open-source materials, with no paywalls or verification required prior to use.

All source files are available on GitHub under open licenses allowing for the use, remixing, and distribution of all materials.

Our wiki describes how community members can contribute to future editions of the library to make our work even more impactful!
